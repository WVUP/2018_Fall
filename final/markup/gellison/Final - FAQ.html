Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I join boosters or buy passes?

A: When you join the Boosters or purchase student passes, you get great admission value and, most importantly, you contribute to the Athletic Boosters' support of our Raiders athletes! All funds raised by the Boosters benefit our athletes directly throughout the year. Your membership and pass purchases are a big portion of reaching our goals!

\

Q: How do I buy spirit wear and signs

A: Visit a local spirit store or vistit their website and order online!

Q: How do I register an athlete?

A: In order for our athletes to play in their seasons, they need to be registered in our database with up-to-date medical and emergency contact information. This is done by pressing the register button at the top of the screen. Once an athlete is registered, information only needs to be updated annually (or where applicable for personal information changes). This database keeps our athletes and teams in compliance with state rules and regulations.

Q: How do I pay fees?

A: Fees can be paid once an athlete is registered by clicking the pay fee link or by visiting the athletics department at the school.
