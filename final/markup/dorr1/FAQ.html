Q: Why get involved with the Booster Club?
A: Because your child's education is important to you! The Booster Club works closely with teachers and administration to make Payne a great academic institution!

Q: What does the Booster Club do for the school?
A: In simple terms: A LOT! The Booster Club is responsible for fundraisers that benefit athletics, teachers, administration and the entire school in general. If the school needs something, we find a way to get it! We support teachers by hosting teacher appreciation events. We get the students involved by hosting school events such as Carnival night. We effectively communicate parent concerns to the administration, and we are an outlet to keep parent-school relations open and successful.

Q: How can I get involved with the Booster Club?
A: There are plenty of ways! You can attend our monthly meetings, volunteer to supervise an event, request to be added to our email list, volunteer your time in any way you can, and so on.

Q: Can I make a donation to the Booster Club?
A: YES! Contact us at RCHSbooster@gmail.com to find out how. All donations are tax deductible!

Q: I don't have a lot of time. Can I still help out?
A: YES! You can help by donating money, or as little time as an hour a semester. We are very flexible and we appreciate any help we can get. We look forward to hearing from you!
