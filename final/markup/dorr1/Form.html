To the Parents of Student Athletes:

The Roane County High School Athletic Booster Club is a non-profit organization comprised of dedicated parents, guardians, alumni, coaches, teachers, school administrators, and community members. The Athletic Booster Club supports ALL teams and athletes at RCHS

Our goals and objectives are to:

  • Help maintain and improve the athletic facilities at RCHS
  • Support and recognize the efforts of our teams and athletes
  • Provide additional opportunities to our teams and athletes not already funded
  • Have on going dialogue with the community, increasing awareness of RCHS Athletic events

Please refer to the news section on the home page to stay update and informed about all the exciting purchases that this club has made to provide the best experience possible for all our student athletes. These items are purchased from our membership dues, fundraisers and corporate sponsorships.


Please help support Athletics at RCHS, join the Athletic Booster Club today!
